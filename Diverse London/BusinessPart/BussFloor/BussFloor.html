The Diversity Scores (SDI Scores) and Composition of Business Floor Space


The map shows the Simpson's Diversity Index (SDI) of different boroughs in business floor space from 2000 to 2012, which is calculated by the sum of the squared proportions of four business floor space types. Formula is as follows: 1 / Sum {PiÂ²}. There are four types of business floor space: Retail, Office, Industry and Other. The total area of each type of business floor space in each borough can be obtained from London Datastore. According to the SDI formula, the SDI scores for business floor space range from 1 (very low diversity) to 4 (very high diversity). What's more, when you hover a borough, the bar chart on the bottom-right corner will update dataset to that borough. Therefore, you can compare the composition of business floor space for a certain borough with the average condition for the entire London.

The pre-processed data employed in this visualization page can be accessed on author's github @zzh-h For more information and comments on this project, please contact author's email: ucbqzz3@ucl.ac.uk
