The Bar Chart Race of London Employment by Different Economic Sectors


This bar chart race aims to show the dynamic trend of the proportions of employees in 16 economic sectors, so as to more intuitively explore the diverse structure of economic sectors across London. The original dataset covers the number of jobs by GLA Economics sectors from 1971 to 2050, which could be obtained from London Datastore. In addition, this series uses employment estimates from 1971 to 1980, quarterly ONS Workforce Jobs data on an annual average basis from 1981 to 2016, and projects employment by sector to 2050. This project calculated the proportion of employees in different sectors, and divides these 16 economic sectors into five categories: Primary Sector, Secondary Sector, Tertiary Sector, Quaternary Sector and Other Services.
